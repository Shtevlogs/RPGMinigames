# Monk: Loss Damage System

**Priority**: 10 (Chunk 2: Combat & Minigames)

## Description
Implement damage taken by Monk when losing an RPS round. Loss effects should deal half enemy attack damage to Monk.

## Requirements
- Calculate damage based on enemy attack damage
- Apply half damage on loss
- Skill attribute reduces loss damage
- Visual feedback for loss damage
