# Equipment Effects on Minigames & Abilities

**Priority**: 24 (Chunk 4: Items, Equipment & Progression)

## Description
Implement equipment effects that modify minigames and abilities. Equipment should change how classes play.

## Requirements
- Equipment modifies minigame parameters (board size, hand size, etc.)
- Equipment adds effect ranges (Berserker)
- Equipment modifies event effects (Time Wizard)
- Equipment modifies card effects (Monk, Wild Mage)
- Equipment provides attribute bonuses
- Equipment creates cross-class synergies (rings)
