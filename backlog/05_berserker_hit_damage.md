# Berserker: Hit Damage System

**Priority**: 05 (Chunk 2: Combat & Minigames)

## Description
Implement damage taken by Berserker when "hitting" (taking another card) in blackjack minigame.

## Requirements

- Damage scales with enemy attack or fixed amount
- Luck attribute reduces hit damage
- Visual feedback for hit damage
